Event Details

Gaming Fest 2026 is an exciting gaming event with a range of tournaments across different game genres. It is open to both casual and competitive players, creating a fun and friendly environment for everyone.

The event takes place over two days at the Scottish Event Campus. There will be different zones for each tournament, making it easy for visitors to find and enjoy the games.

Players can compete for prizes, watch live matches, and meet other gaming fans. Whether you want to play or just watch, there is something for everyone.
