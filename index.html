Gaming Fest 2026

Welcome to Gaming Fest 2026, one of the most exciting gaming events of the year! Join us for two days of esports, entertainment, and fun.

The event will take place at the Scottish Event Campus in Glasgow. It brings together players and fans from across the UK. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced gamer, everyone is welcome.

Enjoy tournaments in popular games like Apex Legends, Valorant, and Super Smash Bros.. You can watch matches, take part, and meet other gamers.

Don’t miss out — book your place and enjoy a great gaming weekend!

Monday 25th May and Tuesday 26th May 2026, from 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Location: Scottish Event Campus

Experience intense tournaments, meet other gamers, and enjoy a vibrant esports atmosphere.
