Super Smash Bros Tournament

The next biggest event will be Super Smash Bros Ultimate, as many people has played it and still does to this day. It is a multiplayer game that revolves around endurance, attrition and quick-thinking strategy to win. This game can be played casually with the max player cap of 8, or played in ranked via Global Smash Power in one-versus-ones.

There are 89 characters to choose from, with additional optional bonuses increasing it to 63, some with different playstyles or the same playstyle, allowing for freedom of choice and what you are comfortable using.

This deviates from the shooter genre in this website, as it focuses on melee combat mostly and tactics you can perform. There are also multiple gamemodes and other things to try that may improve general replayability.
